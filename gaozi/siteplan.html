GAOZI Website Project Site Plan

Site Name

```

GAOZI

GAOZI is a technology-enabled logistics and service platform designed to help individuals, students, and businesses get things done efficiently. The name was chosen because it represents a modern, innovative, and scalable brand that can support logistics, shopping assistance, business services, and productivity tools under a unified platform.

```

Site Purpose

```

GAOZI provides a centralized platform where users can access logistics services, personal shopping assistance, shipment tracking, business delivery solutions, and productivity tools. The website serves as an information hub and service gateway designed to help people complete everyday tasks quickly and conveniently.

```

Scenarios

```
  • How can I send a package across town and track its delivery in real time?
  • Can I hire someone to shop for groceries and deliver them to my location?
  • How can I calculate and monitor my CGPA throughout the semester?
  • What logistics solutions does GAOZI offer for small businesses?
```
